## About This Portfolio

This portfolio demonstrates hands-on GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) capability across **9 domains and 37 controls**, mapped simultaneously to four regulatory and standards frameworks:

- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF 2.0)**
- **ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (Annex A)**
- **NIS2 Directive (EU 2022/2555)**
- **DORA (EU 2022/2554)**

Each deliverable is designed to reflect the type of work a GRC Analyst produces in a real enterprise environment — policy writing, risk assessment, control mapping, vendor risk management, and executive reporting.

## Deliverables

| 01 | **Control Mapping** | 37 controls across 9 domains mapped to NIST CSF 2.0, ISO 27001, NIS2 & DORA with implementation status and gap analysis |

## Frameworks & Mapping Approach

Each control is mapped to its corresponding requirement across all four frameworks simultaneously, demonstrating how a single control satisfies multiple regulatory obligations:

| Framework | Version | Key Focus |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| NIST CSF | 2.0 (2024) | Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover |
| ISO 27001 | 2022 Annex A | Organizational, People, Physical, Technological controls |
| NIS2 | EU 2022/2555 | Risk management, incident handling, supply chain, continuity |
| DORA | EU 2022/2554 | ICT risk, incident reporting, third-party risk, resilience testing |
